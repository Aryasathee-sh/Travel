Nature and Outdoor Activities

Amazing nature and adventure tourism destinations in Brazil.





Home to beautiful landscapes and endless natural resources all over its territory, Brazil is one of the best ecotourism destinations in the world. The amount of activities available for visitors is impressive, including wildlife and nature observation, hiking, sports fishing, horseback riding, cave visits, and much more. Enjoy everything Brazil has to offer.



Belem




A capital do estado do Para e uma das mais antigas do Brasil e e repleta de atrativos, belezas e encantos naturais.

Porto Seguro


Porto Seguro (or just Porto) is famous for its beautiful beaches, remarkable cuisine and incredible leisure centers. The destination is much more than that, though. Those who love contact with nature will find an ecotourism haven.

Buzios


Surrounded by slopes and hills full of Mata Atlantica’s natural beauty, Armacao dos Buzios – or just Buzios – calls upon the visitor’s adventurous spirit and invites them to be in touch with nature.




Porto de Galinhas


A white-sand beach, coconut trees, and crystal-clear waters. The natural beauty of Porto de Galinhas combines all of this and much more. Ecotourism and adventure tourism enthusiasts will love to go on buggy rides around the region and to go diving in the natural pools.

Paraty


Paraty combina belezas naturais, paisagens deslumbrantes e historia. Um destino que fara voce se encantar por esse pedacinho do Brasil.

Manaus


Manaus, a charming and cultural city, is the gateway to the Amazon Rainforest. The green local vegetation, the dark waters of Rio Negro, the muddy waters of Solimões River, exotic local cuisine, diverse architecture, and very welcoming people are some of the city’s treasures. One visit is all it takes to fall in love with the capital of Amazonas State.




Ilha do Mel


Calm, stunning, natural scenery and excellent beaches make up this paradise located in Paranaguá Bay, only 130 km from Curitiba.

Rio de Janeiro


Stunning natural attractions, adventure tourism, and iconic monuments that are renowned all over the world make the city of Rio de Janeiro an incomparable and unforgettable destination.

Jericoacoara


With its calm seas, Jericoacoara is the perfect location to practice water sports and experience amazing natural beauty.
